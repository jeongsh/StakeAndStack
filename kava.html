About Kava

Kava Network is a Layer-1 blockchain that combines Cosmos SDK's speed and scalability with Ethereum's developer support. It enables seamless interoperability, optimized scalability with Tendermint Core consensus, and rapid ecosystem growth. KAVA, the native token, governs and secures the network, fostering Web3 and next-gen blockchain development.
